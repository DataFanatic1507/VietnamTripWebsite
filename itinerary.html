Itinerary: 5N/6D Squad Adventure

Day 1: Arrival + Da Nang
Touchdown in Da Nang. Beach vibes, street food, city walks, squad dinner, and local markets.
Day 2: Hoi An + Nightlife
Day trip to magical Hoi An, lantern-lit streets, street eats, river strolls, epic nightlife.
Day 3: Ba Na Hills
Golden Bridge, cable car views, French village. Squad photos galore. Return to Da Nang.
Day 4: Hanoi Arrival
Fly to Hanoi, explore Old Quarter, Beer Street, culture, and maybe a pub crawl.
Day 5: Ha Long Bay
Full day cruise, kayaking, swimming, stunning limestone islands. Epic squad day!
Day 6: Hanoi + Departure
Final squad brunch, last-minute shopping, and flight home. Trip of a lifetime!
