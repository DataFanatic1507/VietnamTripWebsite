Day 2: Hoi An Day Trip

Fri, 5th September 2025

Morning

  • Leave for Hoi An by 9 AM (Private Grab)
  • Breakfast at Rosie’s Cafe
  • Walk Ancient Town: Japanese Bridge, colorful alleys, photo ops
  • Optional: Get custom-tailored clothes

Afternoon & Evening

  • An Bang Beach (optional)
  • Explore shops, art, local snacks
  • Lantern Boat Ride on Thu Bon River
  • Dinner at Mango Mango
  • Hoi An Night Market
  • Return to Da Nang ~10 PM
Fun fact: Hoi An is a UNESCO World Heritage site. The lanterns, tailor shops, and yellow buildings make it one of Asia’s most Instagrammable towns. It’s also famous for “Banh Mi Phuong,” Anthony Bourdain’s favorite sandwich!
