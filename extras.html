Packing List, Budget & Goofy Squad Stuff

Packing & Prep Checklist

  • Passport + visa (e-visa for India)
  • Vietnamese Dong (cash + Revolut/Niyo card if available)
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, hat
  • Flip flops + walking shoes
  • Power adapter (Type A/C/D – 220V)
  • Light clothes (humid!), 1 beach outfit
  • Waterproof pouch (for Ha Long Bay)
  • Grab app pre-installed
  • Offline Google Maps + Translate
