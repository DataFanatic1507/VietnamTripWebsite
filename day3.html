Day 3: Ba Na Hills Adventure

Sat, 6th September 2025

Full Day

  • 7:30 AM Pickup for Ba Na Hills
  • Cable Car (longest in the world!)
  • Golden Bridge (held by giant hands)
  • French Village, Fantasy Park, Wax Museum
  • Buffet lunch (or DIY food adventure)
  • Return to Da Nang by evening
Fun fact: Ba Na Hills features the world’s longest single cable car (5.8km) and the iconic Golden Bridge, which appears to be held up by two giant stone hands! The area is also 1,487 meters above sea level, with cool air year-round.
