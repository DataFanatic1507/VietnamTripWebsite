Day 4: Hanoi Arrival & Old Quarter

Sun, 7th September 2025

Afternoon

  • Flight to Hanoi (morning)
  • Hoan Kiem Lake, Ngoc Son Temple
  • Phung Hung Art Street
  • Egg Coffee at Cafe Giang (Hanoi specialty!)

Evening

  • Ta Hien Beer Street: party, beer towers, street eats
  • Train Street (watch the train pass up close!)
  • Street food crawl: Bun Cha, Banh Cuon, spring rolls
Fun fact: The Old Quarter is over a millennium old, with winding streets named after the goods they once sold (e.g., Silver Street, Paper Street). Try crossing the street like a local—it’s a rite of passage!
