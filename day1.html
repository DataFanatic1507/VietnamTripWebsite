Day 1: Da Nang Arrival & Beach

September 4, 2025

Morning

  • Flight: Delhi → HCMC → Da Nang (~11 AM arrival)
  • Cab: Grab (~₹1500) to villa near My Khe Beach
  • Chill at the villa
  • Light lunch nearby (try Bún Chả Cá or Banh Xeo)

Evening

  • My Khe Beach: walk, sunset, swim
  • Han River + Dragon Bridge
  • Son Tra Night Market
  • Coffee stop: 43 Factory Coffee Roaster
  • Optional: River cruise on Han (~₹500–₹800)
  • Dinner: Local seafood or Banh Mi
