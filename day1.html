Day 1: Da Nang Arrival & Beach

Thu, 4th September 2025

Morning

  • Arrive in Da Nang (~11 AM)
  • Cab to villa near My Khe Beach
  • Chill at the villa
  • Light lunch nearby (try Bún Chả Cá or Banh Xeo)

Evening

  • My Khe Beach: walk, sunset, swim
  • Han River + Dragon Bridge
  • Son Tra Night Market
  • Coffee stop: 43 Factory Coffee Roaster
  • Optional: River cruise on Han (~₹500–₹800)
  • Dinner: Local seafood or Banh Mi
Did you know? My Khe Beach is rated as one of the most beautiful beaches on the planet by Forbes. It's known for its white sand and gentle waves—perfect for swimming and sunrise photos!
