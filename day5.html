Day 5: Ha Long Bay Cruise

Mon, 8th September 2025

Cruise Day

  • 6:30–7:00 AM Pickup from hotel
  • Drive to Ha Long Bay (3.5 hrs)
  • Kayaking or bamboo boat ride
  • Visit Sung Sot Cave, Titop Island
  • Buffet lunch on board
  • Return to Hanoi by ~8:30 PM
Fun fact: Ha Long Bay means "Descending Dragon Bay." According to legend, dragons descended to protect Vietnam from invaders, creating the limestone islands. The bay is a UNESCO World Heritage Site!
